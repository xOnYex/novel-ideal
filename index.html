Our Mission

Our mission is to encourage out-of-school reading among children and teenagers. In the modern world of internet and smartphones, the youth are increasingly hesitant to put down Twitter and pick up a book. We therefore encourage everyone to take a stand and push towards non-compulsory reading. The future of our society depends on eager-to-learn, literate young adults.

About Us

We're an organization founded in 2022 to promote the idea of reading for fun among children and teens. Our founder, Onyebuchi, was an avid reader as a kid - books like Harry Potter and Gregor the Overlander stole his attention away for hours. Now, he strives to give the youth the opportunity to do the same. To this end, he partnered with the US Department of Education to create the #ReadWhereYouAre movement - just another step towards creating the novel ideal.

Importance

Reading is an important skill to cultivate when one is young. Reading has been shown to increase creativity and attentiveness, both strong attributes to have in our increasingly bustling world. The act of simply stopping and reading can save one hours of work in an attempt to solve problems that are right in front of them. Therefore, we strive to increase the number of avid readers in the world, one kid at a time.
